Equipment Plus is a DELTARUNE mod that aims to tweak existing equipment, re-implement unused items, and add brand new ones!
Currently, only Chapters 1 & 2 feature these changes. Chapters 3 & 4 are included for compatibility, allowing you to carry over and use the new gear from earlier chapters. 
As such, they don’t yet have their own additions. We plan to expand into Chapters 3 & 4 in the future, so stay tuned!

FEATURES:
- Tweaks to existing equipment, such as the Dice Brace and Devilsknife.
- Re-implementation of unused gear, such as the Trefoil, Mouse Token, etc.
- Brand new armor, like the RevivePin!
- More fusions that allow you to craft some of the unused & new equipment!
- SwallowSword and ClownCollar, new weapons for Kris & Ralsei obtained by beating Jevil.
- WingCutlass and HeadSmasher, new weapons for Kris & Susie obtained by beating Spamton NEO.
- Seam's shop updates with loot from each Chapter—if you recruited enough Darkners!
- A new item...?


PLANNED FEATURES:

- More original armor-pieces, like the Mana Band!
